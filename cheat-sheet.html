Cloud PC keeps data inside · Intune manages the device · Entra ID controls access · Copilot Studio = no-code build.
→ This is a slide, not a demo. Don't switch screens.
12:30
2 min

KYC · regulatory reporting · contract review · vendor onboarding. Anywhere there's a screen with no API.
→ Land the message: "The work you gave up on automating — that's where the most hours are being spent."
